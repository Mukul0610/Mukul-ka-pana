About Me :-

I'm a student at IIT Bombay with a passion for all things tech, especially Machine Learning and Deep Learning. My love for these fields drives me to continuously challenge myself and deepen my understanding of the latest advancements.

Aside from my studies, I'm also an entrepreneur at heart and am always on the lookout for new and innovative business opportunities. I've also dabbled in algorithmic trading and have created numerous Expert Advisors (EA) for the Metatrader5 platform. My passion for math only adds to my excitement in this field.

When I'm not coding or trading, I love to unwind by reading books and coming up with new ideas. I'm also an active contributor on Kaggle, where I regularly upload datasets and notebooks to showcase my skills and collaborate with others in the community.

In my free time, I work as a freelancer on Upwork and am always open to new projects. Currently, I'm working on a website for my startup, which I hope will become a platform for me to share my technical skills and entrepreneurial spirit with the world.

Finally, I'm also a blogger who loves to write about a wide range of topics, from technology to life lessons. Writing is a powerful tool for expressing thoughts and ideas, and I hope to inspire others through my words.

So, there you have it! A student, entrepreneur, algo-trader, reader, innovator, freelancer, Kaggler, math geek, and blogger all rolled into one. I'm always up for a challenge and I'm excited to see what the future holds.
